About Us

Study CCC is an innovative platform designed for California Community College students to connect with peers and form study groups. Tackle challenging subjects, prepare for exams, and collaborate on assignments with ease.

Included Schools

  • Los Angeles Pierce College
  • Sacramento City College
  • San Diego Mesa College
  • Fresno City College
  • Santa Monica College
  • Los Angeles Mission College

What We Offer

  • Search for Study Groups: Filter by course, campus, or topic to find the perfect group.
  • Create Your Own Group: Host study sessions and invite classmates to join.
  • Access Study Resources: Share notes, links, and tips within your group.
